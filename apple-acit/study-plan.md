## Practice exam facts (SUP-2026)

- Signup: Apple Account → ACRS → Practice Exams → continue to Pearson VUE → **paid per attempt**
- ~80 scored questions, 120 min, one sitting, pass = 75% (not rounded)
- Fail → 7-day wait + pay again. Take it once, prepared, at the Week 4 gate — not as a casual baseline.
- All Apple exams (practice + certification) run through Pearson VUE / OnVUE — not Kryterion.
