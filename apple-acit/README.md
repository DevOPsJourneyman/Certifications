# Apple Certified IT Professional (ACIT)

**Exams:** Apple Device Support + Apple Deployment and Management
**Provider:** Apple
**Target date:** August 2026

## Exam Breakdown

| Exam | Objectives |
|------|-----------|
| Device Support | 14 objectives — hardware, macOS, Apple ID, iCloud |
| Deployment & Management | 17 objectives — ABM, MDM, network, supervision |
