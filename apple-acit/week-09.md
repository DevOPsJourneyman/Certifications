## DEP-2026 EXAM DAY

### Morning checklist
- [ ] Light meal — nothing heavy
- [ ] Skim top DEP-2026 traps only from `week-07.md` (5 min max)
- [ ] Online: join Kryterion 30 min early; camera/mic check; ID ready; clear desk
- [ ] Test centre: arrive 15 min early
- [ ] No new reading

### Exam strategy
1. Scenario questions: read fully — the situation in the question stem often rules out 2 of 4 answers
2. Watch for questions that contrast Intune vs Apple MDM behaviour (conflict resolution, compliance states, enrollment types) — these are common distractors once you've studied both platforms
3. Flag uncertain questions — answer your best guess, return at end
4. No blank answers
